About Jagriti Shikshan Sansthan

Jagriti Shikshan Sansthan, based in Ajmer, Rajasthan, is dedicated to offering alternative education paths to students who have faced academic setbacks. With over a decade of experience, we provide government-recognized diploma, degree, and vocational courses to help students restart and reshape their future.

Our mission is to ensure that no student feels left behind due to failure or dropout. We empower learners with practical skills, confidence, and a clear path to success in life and career.
